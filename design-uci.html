RecipEZ

Design at UCI

April 2023 - June 2023

Overview : With a team of 6, designed the app prototype called RecipEZ, a recipe app to find recipes and share your own for cash rewards!


Table of Contents

  • Design Question
  • How it Works
  • Final Products
  • Why it Matters

Design Question

How might we foster a community of cooks of all skill levels to provide recipe suggestions and encourage new ones?


Solution

Our solution was to design an application that gamifies a typical recipe app and discover new recipes with difficulty levels.




How it Works

Surveys & Competitive Analysis

Based on our research, we decided to include key features such as Rewards, Reviews & Ratings, and Recipe Posts.


Sketches, Wireframes & Userflows

After research, we began ideating how users would use our app. Then coming up with some low-fidelity sketches and wireframes.


Figma Prototype

After evaluating our wireframes, we created a Figma Prototype to allow users to use and experience RecipEZ as if it were a fully functioning app.
