Design Question

How might we reimagine Hustle N' Code to promote more involvement, interaction and engagement?


Solution

Our solution was to strategically revision two elements of Hustle N’ Code: Post-feature & Comment-feature and add a Notification Center.




How it Works

User Persona

I created a Persona to get an understanding of Hustle N’ Code users. Henry Martinez is a real Hustle N’ Coder and full stack developer.


Storyboard

This storyboard is a depiction of how Henry would interact with a new notification center and comment feature.


Sketches & Wireframes

After understanding wants and needs, I created sketches & wireframes of what the Post, Comment, and Notification Center could look like.
